About Me

Finance as a strategic lever

I grew up in finance believing that numbers tell stories — but only if you ask the right questions. My career has been built on bridging the gap between raw financial data and the boardroom decisions it should inform.

Having worked across multinational corporations and high-growth scale-ups, I bring a rare combination of rigorous analytical depth and the business acumen to make those insights actionable for commercial leaders.

Outside the spreadsheet, I write about FP&A best practices and the evolving role of finance in the AI era — because I believe the most valuable FP&A professionals are the ones who never stop learning.

Clarity Over Complexity
Distilling multi-variable financial scenarios into one clear recommendation for decision-makers.
Forward-Looking Mindset
Moving finance from reporting the past to anticipating the future through driver-based models.
True Business Partnership
Sitting alongside commercial teams — not behind them — to build shared ownership of outcomes.
